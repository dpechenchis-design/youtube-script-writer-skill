# YouTube Script Writer

Writes high-retention, conversion-optimized YouTube scripts using the Dan Martell framework.
Outputs: 3-5 Title options, Thumbnail concepts, and a full script with timestamps.

---

## Input Format

User provides:

```
Тема: [What the video is about]
Ніша/клієнт: [coaching / SaaS / finance / productivity / etc.]
Proof автора: [credentials, results, experience — what gives authority]
Цільова дія: [subscribe / book a call / download / buy / etc.]
Тривалість: [short ~8 min / medium ~12 min / long ~18 min] (optional, default: medium)
```

**If topic or niche is missing — ask before writing.** You need both to write a converting script.

## Output Structure

### 1. PRE-PRODUCTION: 3T

Before the script — generate these elements:

#### TOPIC VALIDATION
- TAM check: Is this topic broad enough? (target: 100K+ potential viewers)
- Search intent: What would someone Google before watching this?
- Competition gap: What angle hasn't been covered well?

#### TITLES (3-5 варіантів)

Generate 3-5 emotional titles that tap into:
- **Fears**: What they're afraid of (failing, missing out, staying stuck)
- **Blocks**: What's stopping them (confusion, overwhelm, wrong approach)
- **Dreams**: What they want (freedom, results, recognition)

Title formula options:
- "How to [RESULT] (Without [PAIN])"
- "The [ADJECTIVE] [THING] That [RESULT]"
- "Why [COMMON BELIEF] Is Keeping You [STUCK]"
- "I [RESULT] in [TIME] — Here's How"
- "[NUMBER] [THINGS] That [CHANGED EVERYTHING]"

Max 60 characters. No clickbait — must deliver on promise.

#### THUMBNAIL CONCEPTS (2-3 варіанти)

Each concept includes:
- **Face expression**: (shocked / confident / pointing / thinking)
- **Text overlay**: 3-5 words MAX (contrasting color)
- **Visual element**: (prop, before/after, number, symbol)

---

### 2. HOOK: First 30 Seconds (3P Formula)

The hook determines if they stay. Use exactly this structure:

#### PROOF (5-10 sec)
Why should they listen to YOU?
- Personal result: "I built a $5M company..."
- Client results: "My clients have collectively..."
- Experience: "After 500 coaching calls..."
- Anti-brag option: "I've made every mistake possible..."

Keep it ONE sentence. Don't oversell.

#### PROMISE (10-15 sec)
What will they GET from watching?
- Specific outcome: "By the end of this video, you'll know exactly how to..."
- Transformation: "...so you can finally stop [PAIN] and start [GAIN]"
- Stakes: Why this matters NOW

Make the promise concrete and measurable when possible.

#### PLAN (5-10 sec)
Three things they'll learn (creates mental structure):
- "I'm going to show you three things..."
- "First... Second... And third..."
- Keep each point to 3-5 words

This is also your CONTENT STRUCTURE — the 3 points become your 3 main sections.

---

### 3. MAIN CONTENT: PEIL Cycles

For EACH of the 3 points from your Plan, use the PEIL framework:

#### P — POINT (15-30 sec)
- State the idea/concept clearly
- Use a memorable phrase or name for it
- "The first thing is what I call [NAME]..."

#### E — EXPLAIN (1-2 min)
- Why does this matter?
- What happens if they ignore this?
- How does it work conceptually?
- Challenge the common misconception

#### I — ILLUSTRATE (1-3 min)
- Personal story that proves the point
- Client case study with specific numbers
- Hypothetical scenario that feels real
- Before/after comparison

Stories > Stats. But stats inside stories = gold.

#### L — LESSON/TEACH (1-2 min)
- Exact steps to implement
- Framework or acronym they can remember
- "Here's what you need to do..."
- Actionable takeaway they can use TODAY

---

### 4. ASYMMETRIC PACING

To maintain retention, use progressive lengthening — each section gets longer:

#### Section Lengths (Progressive Build)
Start fast, then go deeper with each point:
- Point 1: PEIL (shortest, ~1-2 min) — quick win, builds momentum
- Point 2: PEIL (medium, ~2-3 min) — more depth
- Point 3: PEIL (longest, ~3-4 min) — deepest dive, most value

**Why this works:** The short first section creates a sense of speed and progress. As viewers invest more time, they're more committed to finishing. Each block can be slightly longer because they're already hooked.

#### Reengage Loops (every 2-3 min)
Insert "open loops" to keep them watching:
- "In a second, I'll show you the exact template I use..."
- "But first, let me explain why most people get this wrong..."
- "Stay until the end — I'll share the one thing that..."

#### Pattern Interrupts
Break monotony with:
- B-roll callouts: "[Show example on screen]"
- Direct questions: "Have you ever felt this way?"
- Tone shifts: Serious → playful → serious
- Movement cues: "Let me draw this out..."

---

### 5. CLOSE & CTA (60-90 sec)

#### Recap (20-30 sec)
- Quickly restate the 3 points
- "So remember: [Point 1], [Point 2], and [Point 3]"

#### Bridge to CTA (20-30 sec)
- Connect the content to the next step
- "If you found this valuable and want to go deeper..."
- "The next step for you is..."

#### Call to Action (20-30 sec)
ONE primary action. Options:
- Subscribe: "Hit subscribe — I post [frequency] about [topic]"
- Free resource: "I put together a [resource] — link in description"
- Book a call: "If you want help implementing this, book a call..."
- Next video: "Watch this video next [point to card]"

## Tone Rules

**Voice:** Conversational expert — like explaining to a smart friend over coffee

**DO:**
- Short sentences. One idea per line.
- Direct address: "you" not "people" or "viewers"
- Contractions: you're, I'll, that's, don't, won't
- Specific numbers > vague claims
- Stories with sensory details
- Questions that make them think

**DON'T:**
- "In today's video..." (boring)
- "Hey guys, welcome back..." (skip the intro)
- "Without further ado..." (filler)
- Corporate buzzwords: leverage, unlock, skyrocket, synergy
- Passive voice: "It is important to..." → "You need to..."
- Over-explaining: trust the viewer's intelligence

## Duration Guidelines

| Length | Total Time | Hook | PEIL 1 | PEIL 2 | PEIL 3 | Close |
|--------|-----------|------|--------|--------|--------|-------|
| Short | ~8 min | 30s | 1 min | 2 min | 3 min | 60s |
| Medium | ~12 min | 30s | 2 min | 3 min | 4-5 min | 90s |
| Long | ~18 min | 30s | 2-3 min | 4-5 min | 6-7 min | 90s |

**Pattern:** First block fastest → each subsequent block longer (progressive immersion)

## Retention Checklist

Before finalizing:

- [ ] Hook creates curiosity in first 5 seconds
- [ ] Proof is specific, not generic
- [ ] Promise is concrete and measurable
- [ ] Each PEIL section has a named concept
- [ ] At least one story/example per point
- [ ] Open loops every 2-3 minutes
- [ ] Section lengths vary (asymmetric pacing)
- [ ] One clear CTA at the end
- [ ] No filler — every line earns its place
- [ ] Reads naturally when spoken aloud
